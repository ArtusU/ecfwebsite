About Us

Edinburgh Community Food is a social enterprise and a charity. Our social enterprise work is all about sourcing and selling the best quality fruit and vegetables. We run stalls in Edinburgh’s hospitals selling our great produce to staff, patients and visitors. We also deliver fresh fruit and vegetables to homes and businesses across the city. All our profits go back into our charitable work.
